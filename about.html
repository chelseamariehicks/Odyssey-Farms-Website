Odyssey Farm

About

We're Oliver and Chelsea, the passionate partnership behind Odyssey Farm.

After teaching overseas at international schools for nearly a decade, we repatriated to the Pacific Northwest to embark on the ambitious project of developing a sustainable, organic vegetable farm and arts event space in the impressive, forested Oregon Coast range.

Our 16-acre piece of land butts up against vast acres of forest land and includes multiple freshwater ponds and a creek running through to the Nehalem River. The original structure on the property was built on stumps in the 1920s and used as a ticketing checkpoint for truck drivers transporting lumber. After pouring tremendous amounts of sweat equity into the ramshackle home, we have transformed the structure into a shared space for hosting workshops, intimate concerts and creative performances, as well as farm-to-table dinner parties with produce grown on our land.

In addition to the main house and our farming operation, we've also built several quaint, tiny-homes on the property to host artists in residence and guests eager for a break from the hustle and bustle of city life.

We encourage you to visit our oasis in the Pacific Northwest wonderland on your next outing to the Oregon Coast.
